Welcome to the Internet home of London Fields Cricket Club.

We're a community cricket club based in Hackney, North East London We are members of the North East London Cricket League and also play a large amount of friendlies across the summer months. We predominantly play our cricket in the Hackney borough.

We are always on the lookout for new talent, so if you are interested in any form of cricket, please free to connect with us via the club email available in the About page.
